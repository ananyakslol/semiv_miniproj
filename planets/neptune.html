Neptune

Eighth Planet from the Sun

Overview

Neptune is the eighth and farthest known planet from the Sun. It is an ice giant with a deep blue color due to methane in its atmosphere. Neptune has the strongest winds in the solar system[7].

Supersonic Winds

Winds on Neptune can reach up to 2,100 km/h (1,300 mph), the fastest in the solar system[7].

Composition

Neptune is composed mainly of hydrogen, helium, and ices of water, ammonia, and methane.

Atmosphere

80% hydrogen, 19% helium, 1.5% methane

Interior

Water, ammonia, and methane ices

Core

Rock and ice

Exploration

1989

Voyager 2 Flyby

Voyager 2 is the only spacecraft to visit Neptune, discovering new moons and the "Great Dark Spot"[7].

Fun Facts

Dynamic Atmosphere

Neptune's storms come and go, including the famous Great Dark Spot.

Many Moons

Neptune has 14 known moons, including Triton, which orbits backward.

Ice Giant

Neptune is called an "ice giant" because of its icy interior.
